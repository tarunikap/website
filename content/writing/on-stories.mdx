# on stories

***the main character usually gets a happy ending.***  
***i keep thinking about the good ones who didn't.***

When I watch a movie or read a book, the main character usually gets a happy ending. Things work out. Eventually.

I keep thinking about the good ones who didn't.

Once that thought settles in, stories feel a little different. Even romcoms. Not sad, just slightly off. Like they're showing one outcome and quietly ignoring the rest.

In stories, it is obvious who the protagonist is. The camera follows them. The narrative makes their decisions feel central.

Real life is less clear. Everyone thinks they are the main character. Most days, you are just doing your part and hoping it matters.

There is no background score telling you that you are on the right track. No narrator confirming that this will all make sense later.

You only realise later which moments mattered and which ones changed you without asking. Real life does not pause to tell you this is the important part.

You are in it  
Hope you are paying attention.
