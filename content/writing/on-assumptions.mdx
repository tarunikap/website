# on assumptions

***people notice one thing***  
***and decide they know the whole story.***

People are quick. One detail is usually enough.

Someone does well in one area and that becomes their whole identity. That is what they care about. That is who they are. Everything else fades into the background.

Someone falls short once and that sticks too. One number, one moment, one version. Somehow it turns into a full description.

It does not take much for a label to settle in.

The strange part is the certainty. No one thinks they are guessing. They think they are just seeing clearly.

But most of what makes a person does not show up all at once. It is layered. It changes. It depends on where you are looking from.

Noticing is easy.

Concluding is easier.

Understanding takes time.
