# on extremes

***we call it weird***  
***until it looks familiar***

People act strange when they are too high or too low.

Too excited and they say things they do not mean. Too hurt and they go quiet. Too stressed and they snap. Too happy and they promise things they cannot keep.

From the outside, it looks dramatic. Unnecessary. A bit much.

We react to it. We question it. Sometimes we confront it.

It is easier to point at someone else's imbalance.

Then one day you catch yourself doing something similar. Not the same action. Just the same shift.

Saying too much. Saying nothing. Pulling away. Holding on too tightly.

In the moment, it feels justified. Logical, even.

From the outside, it probably looks the same.

Maybe that is the part we miss.

It's different when you're inside it.

Very different outside.

We often forget that part.
