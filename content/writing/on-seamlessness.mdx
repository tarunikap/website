# on seamlessness

***it looks easy***  
***from the other side of the screen***

A backflip in three steps.  
A new cuisine in five ingredients.  
A skill in one short tutorial.

Everything sounds simple.

Just follow this.  
Just do that.  
Just try once.

The video is smooth. The transitions are clean. The result looks effortless.

What you don't see is how many tries it took before it looked that way.

The failed flips. The burnt versions. The cuts that didn't make it into the final clip.

Online, everything feels compressed. The struggle is edited out. The learning curve is trimmed.

So it starts to look seamless.

Maybe the ease is not fake.  
It is just pre-rehearsed.
