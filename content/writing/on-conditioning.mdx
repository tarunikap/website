# on conditioning

***we laughed at the dogs***  
***turns out we learned faster***

Pavlov rang a bell. The dogs learned that food would follow.  
After a while, the bell alone was enough.

We scroll.

That is the bell.

A sound plays. A tune starts. Our thumb slows down without thinking. We already know the format. We already know how it ends. We already know how to react.

The reward is not food. It is attention. A like. A reply. A small rush that feels automatic.

After some time, the scroll alone is enough.

Even without the phone in your hand, the rhythm stays in your head. You replay it. You expect it.

The strange part is how normal it feels.

We think we are choosing what to watch.  
Sometimes we are just responding.

No leash.  
Same reflex.
