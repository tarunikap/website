# About me

hi, i'm tarunika.

i'm a final year instrumentation and control engineering student at nit trichy. this page is just a place for my writing. random thoughts, small ideas, stuff i don't want to forget.

i like keeping things simple. even the words. but i overthink a lot, so writing kind of happens naturally. it helps me slow down. talking feels fast. writing feels calmer.

otherwise, i keep life pretty quiet.  
music isn't really my thing, but i get why people like it. it's nice background noise, after dogs and books.

i work best when life is predictable. i can't sleep without finishing a series i've started. real life has enough surprises already. once in a while, i like something fast or intense, just to mix things up a bit. i don't really follow trends or worry about how i'm supposed to be.

cutting out the noise has made things easier. i might come off as simple, but my brain is usually busy, and more logical than loud.

some of what i write is technical. a lot of it isn't.

my life isn't very dramatic, so this is where i think things through. sometimes by writing things as they are, sometimes by making stuff up and calling it fiction.

i also had a failed career in chess. it didn't work out. i'm giving it another go now, slowly, and enjoying it more this time.

that's about it.
